Professional Profile

About Me

I am a versatile professional with a natural ability to connect with people and solve problems across diverse contexts—from clinical psychology settings to psychosocial street projects, museums, galleries, and hotel kitchens. I adapt quickly to any client or situation, always prioritizing psychological well-being for clients, colleagues, and team harmony.

My professional foundation is rooted in the Person-Centered Approach of Carl Rogers, which I learned directly from my mentor, psychologist and Jesuit priest Father Evaristo Vasconcelos—a devoted disciple of Rogers. I believe that meaningful human connection, empathic presence, and guidance from experienced mentors matter far more than formal qualifications alone.
